01 — Technology

Flexure units that
think in geometry

Our compliant mechanisms redistribute pressure through shape, not motors. No electronics. No actuators. No failure modes — just structures that deform intelligently under load.

Validated across three generations in FEA simulation, 3D-printed test beds, and clinical trials with diabetic and post-surgical patients.

Applications

One platform,
infinite surfaces

From the sole of a shoe to the joint of a humanoid — compliant mechanisms engineered for every biological load path.

01

Therapeutic Footwear

Shape-morphing insoles for diabetic foot, plantar fasciitis, and post-surgical rehabilitation. Real-time pressure redistribution without electronics.

Entry Market · Active
02

Prosthetic Sockets

Adaptive socket liners that redistribute interface pressure for above- and below-knee amputees, improving comfort and reducing skin breakdown.

Development
03

Wound Care

Offloading devices for pressure ulcer prevention in hospital and elderly care settings, eliminating electronic complexity from critical care pathways.

Pipeline
04

Exoskeleton Joints

Compliant joint elements for lower-limb exoskeletons that absorb shock and adapt to gait variation without active control systems.

Research
05

Humanoid Robotics

Passive compliant feet and manipulator fingers for humanoid robots — mechanical intelligence that reduces actuation demands and improves contact stability.

Vision

Elderly Care

Fall-prevention insoles and support surfaces for long-term care facilities, requiring zero calibration and no battery changes.
